About Me

Hello, I'm Rainie Chi, a curious and driven individual on a mission to merge creativity with coding. As a first-generation immigrant from Taiwan, I've embraced the power of exploration and adaptability from a young age. At 15, I embarked on an exchange student adventure that ignited my love for learning and embracing new cultures.

This journey laid the foundation for a transformative path ahead. Discovering my passion for STEM during my time as an exchange student in the USA, I realized that technology held the key to unlocking my curiosity. This realization set the course for my higher education journey at the University of Washington Tacoma, where I embraced Washington as my new home.

With an INTJ personality driving my pursuits, I approach every challenge with a blend of determination and a thirst for knowledge. The dynamic nature of software engineering fuels my enthusiasm, providing a canvas for both logical problem-solving and innovative thinking. I thrive in academic settings, but I'm also eagerly anticipating the chance to translate my knowledge into real-world impact.

Beyond the realm of coding, I'm creative at heart. Drawing and crocheting are my creative outlets, allowing me to infuse my work with a touch of imagination. As a devoted Disney and Star Wars enthusiast, you'll often find me indulging in yet another rewatch of Revenge of the Sith or meticulously planning my annual trip to a Disney park.
